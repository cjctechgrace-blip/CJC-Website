Our leadership

Prophet Yosef Yifru

Senior Pastor of Christ Jesus Centered Church

Prophet Yosef is a scripture-rooted leader of CJC who embodies the character of Jesus Christ from the New Testament.  His ministry is founded on the promises of God in Exodus 34:10, trusting in the Lord’s covenant to do marvelous works among His people.  He often references passages such as Romans 12:2, urging believers to pursue transformed minds and steadfast faith through diligent spiritual growth.  By weaving these timeless truths into his sermons, Prophet Yosef connects biblical wisdom to the real challenges and joys of everyday human life.

Pastor Emanuel King

Pastor of Christ Jesus Centered Church

Pastor Emanuel is a dedicated minister whose life reflects a transformative journey of faith in Jesus Christ, rooted in His teachings and love.  Embodying John 13:34-35, he teaches believers to love one another as a living testimony of true discipleship.  Guided by Psalm 32:8, he emphasizes seeking divine instruction and allowing God's counsel to shape one’s choices and path of righteousness.  As the leader of the English services at CJC, he devotes himself to spreading the Gospel with biblical wisdom, revelation, and Christ-centered teaching in every aspect of life.
